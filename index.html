DAILY THANTHI

Daily Thanthi (commonly referred to as "Dina Thanthi") is one of the most popular and widely read Tamil-language daily newspapers in India.







THE HINDU

The Hindu is one of India's most respected and widely read English-language newspapers. Founded in 1878, it has a long history of delivering high-quality journalism and in-depth analysis on a variety of topics, including national and international news, politics, business, sports, entertainment, and culture.







DINAMALAR

Dinamalar is one of the most prominent and widely read Tamil newspapers, serving the Tamil-speaking population both in India and around the world.







DECCAN CHRONICLE

Deccan Chronicle is one of India's leading English-language newspapers, with a significant presence in the southern part of the country. It provides comprehensive coverage of news, politics, business, sports, entertainment, and lifestyle topics. Established in 1938, it has a rich history and has evolved to become a trusted source of information for millions of readers.







MAORAMMA

Maoramma is a prominent Malayalam newspaper that has been serving the Malayalam-speaking community with credible and comprehensive news coverage.







RASHTRIYA SAHARA

Rashtriya Sahara is a widely recognized Hindi-language daily newspaper published by the Sahara India Pariwar. Known for its extensive coverage of national and international news, the newspaper has carved a niche for itself in the Hindi-speaking regions of India.
